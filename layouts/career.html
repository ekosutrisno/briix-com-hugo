Software Quality Assurance Automation Engineer

This position is responsible for ensuring software quality across the organization and achieving high automation test coverage.

Responsibilities
  • To plan, design and execute test scenarios to ensure high-quality applications.
  • To develop and maintain scripts for the purpose of QA automation.
  • To identify software application weaknesses and target areas.
  • To participate in requirements review and understand new feature development.
  • To communicate technical concepts/issues clearly to internal stakeholders.
  • To develop, document, and maintain test cases and validation reports.
Qualifications
  • 3+ years total experience in software QA, and at least 1 year in QA automation.
  • Comfortable with working in a dynamic, fast-paced, changing environment.
  • Strong customer focus, excellent problem solving, and analytical skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills.
  • Good command of written English.

Backend Engineer (Java)

We are looking for a Backend Developer who is motivated to develop the backend systems of our mobile and web applications using pragmatic microservices and clean code architecture. These services will need to be designed with scalability in mind and used by hundreds of thousands (and eventually millions) of users.

Responsibilities
  • Develop API-based (REST and GraphQL) services based on requirements.

  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.

  • 3+ years total experience building web apps using Java 8+ with Spring Boot or Quarkus.
